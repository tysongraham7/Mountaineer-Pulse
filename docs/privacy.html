Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Mountaineer Pulse is built to be simple and private. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. There are no accounts, no logins, and no tracking.

Information we collect

None that identifies you. The app does not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal information, and it contains no advertising or third-party analytics or tracking software.

Information stored on your device

Your chosen favorite sports (used to order the home screen) are saved only on your device. This preference never leaves your phone and is not sent to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Information the app displays

Mountaineer Pulse shows publicly available West Virginia University sports information — scores, schedules, rosters, roster movement, and news headlines — aggregated from public sources. News items link out to the original publisher’s website; we show only the headline and source and never republish full articles.

How the app connects

To display this information, the app fetches read-only data from our hosting provider (Supabase). As with any internet service, our hosting and content-delivery providers may automatically process basic technical connection data (such as an IP address) for security and reliability. This is standard server operation, is not used to identify or profile you, and we do not combine it with any personal data.

Third-party links

When you tap a news headline, you leave the app and open a third-party website that has its own privacy practices. We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of those sites.

Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.
